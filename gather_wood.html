Into the Forest
You head toward the cluster of oak trees. Their leaves rustle peacefully overhead, and sunlight filters through the canopy in golden shafts. The forest floor is carpeted with fallen leaves and tall grass. You approach the nearest tree and examine its sturdy trunk - perfect for gathering wood. But movement catches your eye deeper in the forest. Through the trees, you spot wooden structures - a village! Smoke rises from chimneys. You could gather materials here, or investigate that settlement.
