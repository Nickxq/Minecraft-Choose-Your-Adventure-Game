The Village
You emerge from the forest and approach the village. Wooden houses with thatched roofs cluster around a central well. You see villagers walking about - some tending crops, others trading at market stalls. But you notice the sun's position. It's getting low on the horizon. You have a choice: approach now while it's still light and villagers are active, or wait a bit. But if you wait too long and approach after dark, the village's Iron Golem guardian might mistake you for a hostile mob...
