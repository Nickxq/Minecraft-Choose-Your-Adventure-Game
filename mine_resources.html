Mining Deep
You venture into the small cave, wooden pickaxe in hand. Stone breaks easily, and you gather cobblestone for a furnace. As you mine deeper, you spot coal ore embedded in the wall - jackpot! Time passes quickly underground. Suddenly you realize the light from the cave entrance has changed from golden to orange to dark purple. The sun is setting. You hear groaning sounds echoing from deeper in the cave. Do you push your luck and keep mining for more resources? Or head back to the surface now while you still can?
